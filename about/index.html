About

I am a PhD student in the Neurophysics department at the Max Planck Institute for Human Cognitive and Brain Sciences (MPI-CBS) in Leipzig, Germany. I studied physics at the Free University in Berlin and at the Leipzig University, where I extended my studies to musicology and philosophy. During my Master studies in physics, I joined the Neurophysics department at the MPI-CBS to write my Master Thesis in the field of high-resolution fMRI at ultra-high magnetic field strengths. I continue working in this research field during my PhD.
